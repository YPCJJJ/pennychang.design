TS Bank

Digital Banking Experience

UX Designer 2024 Digital Banking

Project Overview

Redesigned the digital banking experience for TS Bank, focusing on improving user engagement, transaction efficiency, and overall customer satisfaction through modern UX design principles.

Challenge

Transform traditional banking interfaces into intuitive, user-friendly digital experiences that cater to both tech-savvy and traditional banking customers.

Solution

Created a modern, accessible banking platform with streamlined navigation, clear information hierarchy, and personalized user experiences.

Impact

Increased mobile banking adoption by 35% and improved customer satisfaction scores from 3.2 to 4.1 out of 5.

Research & Design Process

1. User Research & Analysis

Conducted comprehensive research with existing TS Bank customers to understand their banking habits, pain points, and digital literacy levels.

  • Customer interviews and surveys
  • Usability testing of existing platform
  • Competitive analysis of leading banking apps
  • Behavioral analytics review

2. Information Architecture

Redesigned the information architecture to prioritize frequently used features and create intuitive navigation paths for different user types.

  • User journey mapping
  • Feature prioritization matrix
  • Navigation structure optimization
  • Content organization strategy

3. Interface Design

Developed a clean, modern interface that maintains brand consistency while improving usability and accessibility standards.

  • Responsive design system
  • Accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1)
  • Interactive prototypes
  • Design system documentation

Key Features

Smart Dashboard
