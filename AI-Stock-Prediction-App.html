AI Stock Prediction App

UX Research & Design

UX Designer & Researcher 2024 Mobile App

Project Overview

This project involved designing an AI-powered stock prediction application that helps users make informed investment decisions through machine learning algorithms and intuitive user experience design.

Challenge

Create a user-friendly interface for complex financial data and AI predictions while maintaining trust and transparency in investment decisions.

Solution

Designed an intuitive mobile app with clear data visualization, simplified AI explanations, and comprehensive user education features.

Impact

Improved user engagement by 40% and reduced support tickets by 60% through better UX design and clearer information architecture.

Research & Design Process

1. User Research

Conducted interviews with 25 retail investors to understand their pain points, decision-making processes, and trust factors when using financial apps.

  • User interviews and surveys
  • Competitive analysis of existing apps
  • Behavioral analysis of investment patterns

2. Information Architecture

Designed a clear hierarchy that separates AI predictions from traditional market data, ensuring users understand the difference between automated insights and manual analysis.

  • Card-based layout for different data types
  • Progressive disclosure of complex information
  • Clear labeling of AI-generated content

3. Visual Design

Created a clean, professional interface that builds trust while maintaining visual interest through data visualization and micro-interactions.

  • Consistent color system for different data types
  • Interactive charts and graphs
  • Smooth animations for better engagement

Key Features

AI Prediction Dashboard

Centralized view of all AI-generated stock predictions with confidence levels and historical accuracy.

Risk Assessment Tool

Interactive tool that helps users understand their risk tolerance and portfolio diversification.

Educational Content

Built-in learning modules to help users understand AI predictions and make informed decisions.

Portfolio Tracking

Comprehensive portfolio management with performance analytics and AI-driven insights.

Design Deliverables

User Personas

Detailed profiles of target users including their goals, pain points, and behavioral patterns.

User Journey Maps

Visual representation of user interactions from app discovery to successful investment decisions.

Wireframes & Prototypes

Low-fidelity wireframes and interactive prototypes for user testing and stakeholder review.

Design System

Comprehensive design system including components, patterns, and guidelines for future development.
